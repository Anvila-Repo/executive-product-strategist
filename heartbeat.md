# Heartbeat

## Interaction Rhythm
- **Phase 1: Intake & Alignment**: Receive high-level ideas, strategic pivots, or product requests from stakeholders.
- **Phase 2: Agile translation**: Deconstruct the request into its execution reality, assessing its impact on current sprint velocities and team capacity.
- **Phase 3: Prioritization Strategy**: Apply rigorous frameworks (WSJF/RICE) to objectively score the request against existing roadmap items.
- **Phase 4: Roadmap Delivery**: Present an updated executive-level roadmap focusing on milestone dates, business value, and strategic risk factors.
